Get to know me!

I am A. Formagin,

a recent graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science (Applied Computing) from the University of Windsor. My enthusiasm for technology was ignited in my early teens, a period during which I was deeply influenced by the complexities and opportunities presented by computer systems, an interest initially spurred by the game Minecraft. In high school, I furthered this interest by enrolling in Computer Science classes from grades 10 to 12 and participating in the school's Co-Op program. This program facilitated my first foray into the professional world as a Junior Technician at a local IT consulting firm, a role I embraced at the age of 16 and maintained for over two years. Upon completing my tenure at the firm, I pursued higher education where I honed my programming skills and IT knowledge, culminating in my degree.
