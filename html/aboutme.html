Quick Info

My name is Austin Formagin, and I am a 20 year Computer Science student at the University of Windsor. I started my journey into technology when I began developing an interest in computers around the age of 11 or 12 mostly due to a popular game at the time, Minecraft. Once I was in Highschool I started to take Computer Science classes from grades 10 to 12 and participated in my school's Co-Op program. Through the co-op program, I was able to get my first job as a Juniour Technician at a local IT consulting firm at 16. I spent around 2.5 years working for the firm before I moved onto University where I pursued my love of programming and IT by going for a Bachelor's of Computer Science (Applied Computing).
